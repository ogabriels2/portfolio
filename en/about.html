My Story

Design, code and technology —
from the visual to the backend.

Hi! My name is Gabriel Silva Dias Moreira. I'm a multidisciplinary professional, graduated in Multimedia and currently pursuing a degree in Information Technology (IT) at UNIVESP.

My path has been defined by curiosity. I started with design and audiovisual production, then dove deep into server infrastructure — and naturally became a Full Stack developer. For 5 years I built and maintained in production the Força Aliada ecosystem: a REST API in Node.js + Express, a PostgreSQL database, an AI-moderated social network (Google Vision), an Electron desktop app, and an institutional website.

I'm not just an executor; I'm a system builder. I understand the full cycle — from visual identity to backend, from infrastructure to deployment. My goal is to apply this hybrid vision across design, development and technology to create solutions that genuinely work.
