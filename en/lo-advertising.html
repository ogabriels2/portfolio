Lô Advertising

Branding, Visual Identity & Communication

Project

Academic (Thesis)

Year

2022

Area

Branding & Design

Developed as a Capstone Thesis (TCC) at the renowned Liceu de Artes e Ofícios de São Paulo, Lô Advertising was born as an experimental agency project with a clear purpose: to communicate Brazil's cultural diversity through an accessible yet sophisticated visual language.

The visual identity fuses the classic elegance of Art Nouveau and Art Deco with a warm, welcoming color palette. I was responsible for the entire brand structure, creating a visual system that is simultaneously premium and inclusive, reflecting Brazil's diversity in every detail — from typography to stationery.

The Brand

A fusion of classic styles with a modern geometric approach.

Visual System

Elegant typography and a color palette inspired by Brazilian nature.

Applications

The brand universe applied to premium stationery and merchandise.
